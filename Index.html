Questions

Frequently asked

How long does setup take?+
Most institutions are fully set up within one business day. We provide an onboarding guide and data import templates for students, teachers, and courses. No developer needed.
Can we import our existing student data?+
Yes. EduFlow supports CSV and Excel imports for students, teachers, courses, and historical grades. Our import wizard validates your data before committing any changes.
Is student data secure and FERPA compliant?+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256 / TLS 1.3). We are FERPA compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified, and never share data with third parties.
Can different staff have different access levels?+
Yes. EduFlow has role-based access control (RBAC) with predefined roles for Admin, Registrar, Faculty, and Student. Each role sees only the features and data relevant to them.
Do you offer a free trial for Pro or Enterprise?+
Yes — Pro comes with a 30-day free trial, no credit card required. Enterprise plans include a guided pilot program. Contact our sales team to get started.
What support options are available?+
Starter plans have access to our documentation and community forum. Pro includes priority email support with 24-hour response times. Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.
